Deleting a Passkey

Your passkey has been successfully deleted by the server, but you must now delete it on your device.

Follow these steps in order to do so:

On iOS:

Click the link below to open the Passkeys section in your iOS settings:

Go to Settings

Next Steps

  1. Log in with Face ID or Touch ID
  2. In the search-bar at the top, search for accounts.designø.com
  3. Then on the selected passkey, click on info (i) then Delete Passkey

For Android:

  1. Open the Chrome app on your Android device.
  2. Tap on the three dots in the top-right corner to open the menu.
  3. Tap on Settings.
  4. Tap on Passwords.
  5. Find Designø.com and delete the passkey.
